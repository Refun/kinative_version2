ABOUT CLIENT

  • Business Name: Event Staffing Network

  • Client: Ellen Euen

  • Origin: United States

  • Project Runtime: February - April 2017

ESN is an American Start up compnay that provides event management solu-tion. It is a centralized platform where industry professionals can acquire or pro-vide services for a successful event--staffing and production. Clients can hire agencies to run events, agencies can hire talents and vendors from the large da-tabase of professional talents and vendors and keep track of every steps
